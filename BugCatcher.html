Bug Catcher

EVENT DESCRIPTION

Test your code debugging skills at Bug Catcher, where you will not only be challenged by competitive coding skills but by a vital subset of it; attention to detail. If you pride yourself in catching details hidden under lines of code and logic, this is the perfect event to prove your skills and claim your rightful prize..

EVENT DETAILS

The event will comprise of two rounds. Each team will be playing in both the rounds.

Round 1:

Round 1 will be paper based where each team will be provided with codes on paper. There will be three to four questions which will have logical as well as syntax errors. The language used will be C++ because most of the programmers are familiar with it. The time allotted for this round in approximately 40 minutes A five minute break will be given after the first round.

Round 2:


Round 2 will be on compiler “g++”, where the code will be provided to each team on spot. Participants have to identify the errors in the code, correct them so it to gives a proper output and submit the file. Round 2 will be of approximately 1 hour. At the end of Round 1, the Question for Round 2 will be announced to all the participants and they will be given 15 minutes break to discuss the problem with their team members and search online for the algorithm. Use of Internet will only be allowed for 15 minutes and it will be ensured by the Management team that the participants cannot go through any code over the internet but only the helping algorithms.

RULES AND REGULATION:

Round 1 will be paper based while Round 2 will be compiler based.
Participants are not allowed to use any debugging tools whatsoever or any other helping device during. Doing so would lead to disqualification.
Participants can only communicate with their team members and with the Management team in case of any queries.
Use of Mobile Phones is not allowed during the competition.
Use of Internet is not allowed during the competition.
Participants will not be allowed to go out of the room during the competition.
Management team holds the right to change any rule without any prior notice.
Decision of judges cannot be challenged and will be considered final

TIME DURATION:

The time Duration for the event is two hours.

TEAM SIZE:

2 participants at max are allowed.
