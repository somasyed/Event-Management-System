Quiz Competition

EVENT DESCRIPTION

The Quiz Competition can also be called a mind sport which allows teams to demonstrate their knowledge in computer science and computing technology by answering questions. This competition will offer a chance for participants to gain substantial experience, showcase skills, analyze and evaluate outcomes and uncover personal aptitude.

EVENT DETAILS

The event consists of three rounds. In each round, teams are screened out and only teams which qualify move forward to the next round.

Round 1:

● Each team will be required to solve 100 multiple choice questions.
● There will be 60 minutes to solve these 100 multiple choice questions.
● The teams will then be evaluated and the top 8 teams will be promoted to the second round.

Round 2:


● This round will be played by top 8 teams that qualify from the first round.
● Teams will be given 20 minutes to solve 60 questions.
● Negative marks will be given for incorrect answers.
● 2 teams with the maximum number of points will qualify for the grand final round.

Round 3:


● This round is called the ‘ buzzer round
● This round will be played by the top 2 team.
● Questions will be asked and the team that presses the buzzer first will have the chance to answer the question.
● If a team answers incorrectly, the opposing team will get a chance to steal and answer the question.
● The team with the highest scores at the end of this round will be declared the winner to the competition.

RULES AND REGULATION:


● Participants can only communicate with their team members and with the Management team in case of any queries.
●Use of Mobile Phones is not allowed during the competition.
● Use of Internet is not allowed during the competition.
● Participants will not be allowed to go out of the room during the competition.
● Management team holds the right to change any rule without any prior notice.
● Decision of judges cannot be challenged and will be considered final

TIME DURATION:

The time Duration for the event is two hours.
Round1: 1 hours
Round2: 20 minutes
Round3: 2 hours
Total Event Duration: 30 minutes

TEAM SIZE:

2 Participants
