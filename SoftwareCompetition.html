Software Project Competition

EVENT DESCRIPTION

The competition is a 2 day event that requires its participants to fulfill some important requirements in order to compete. It includes:
i) Abstract of project. (maximum 1 5 lines)
ii) Description (max 400 500 words) of software that explain about software, technology, and platform used in project.
iii) Design of the application. Section includes complete diagrams of system design and architecture.
iv) Applications of the software. Complete details of use, benefits and targeted users of the software
A prototype of your project.

EVENT DETAILS

The Event provides its contestants to develop a mobile application on any of the three Mobile OS including Android, IOS and Windows. Each Team would be given a theme or general problem and they would implement their ideas to solve that problem with a Mobile Application provided by the Mobile Application Team. The theme/task would be told to all the participants before the competition starts. Internet will be provided to the participants during the competition to download and use resources. Hybrid Apps using Xamarin, Ionic Framework, React JS etc are not allowed. Android Apps for Java using Android SDK and Swift and Objective C are allowed for iOS apps.

RULES AND REGULATION:

All member of a team/group should be enrolled in an undergraduate degree program.
●A team may consist of minimum one and maximum of five members.
●At least one member of team must be available during the competition at the entry display.
●Projects must be on display during event time.
●Display of shields and awards are not allowed.
●Participants should arrange their own hardware (systems, mouse, keyboard, and Internet).
●Poster (size A0 or A1, portrait or landscape) are allowed.
●Teams are responsible for their belongings.
●If any software found copy or replica of any other software, the act will be considered plagiarism and team will be disqualified.
●Decision of judges shall be considered final and can’t be challenged.
●The team reserves the rights to disqualify any team who fail to adhere to the mentioned rules.
●The team reserves the right to modify any rule without prior notice.
